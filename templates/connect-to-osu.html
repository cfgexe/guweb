Here are two ways to join {{ domain() }} - using our switcher or create a osu! shortcut.


Shortcut instructions:

  1. Create a new Shortcut from the osu!.exe game file or create a copy of your existing osu! shortcut.
  2. Right-click the Shortcut and go on Properties.
  3. Add -devserver {{ domain() }} to the Target, right after your directory.
  4. Your changes will look like - E:\Games\osu!\osu!.exe -devserver {{ domain() }}
  5. Click on Apply.
  6. Enjoy!

If you have any issues, feel free to contact us through our discord!
